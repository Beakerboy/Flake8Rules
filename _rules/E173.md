---
code: E173
message: "Missing whitespace after arithmetic operator"
title: "Missing whitespace after arithmetic operator (E173)"
---

There should be one space before and after an arithmetic operator (`+`, `-`, `/`, `*`, `^`, `\\` and `Mod`).

### Anti-pattern

```vba
Bar = 3 +2
```

### Best practice

```vba
Bar = 3 + 2
```
